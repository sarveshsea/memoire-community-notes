# Audit accessibility

Read [the accessibility audit guide](references/guide.md). Establish the supported standard, product surface, browsers, assistive technologies, and critical journeys. Combine automated checks with keyboard, zoom, screen-reader, pointer, and reduced-motion testing. Cite exact locations and user impact, separate confirmed defects from manual-review needs, and prioritize blockers to task completion. Re-test every fix in the affected journey.
