# Analyze interview research

Read [the interview analysis guide](references/guide.md). Confirm consent, participant labeling, redaction needs, and the decision the research should inform. Preserve verbatim evidence separately from interpretation, code consistently across participants, and surface contradictions and sampling limits. Do not fabricate quotes or demographics. Deliver traceable themes, supporting excerpts within copyright and privacy limits, and implications for the product decision.
